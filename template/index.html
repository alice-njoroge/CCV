Welcome to CCV website:

Who are we?

We are a group of volunteers who help in conservation Kenya’s largest population of Grey Crowned Cranes, which are globally endangered birds. This is done by engaging and improving the lives of our local community who share the cranes’ home around Lake Ol’Bolossat

Where are we located?

We are located in Central Kenya. Our major areas of operation and concern are around Lake Ol’Bolossat, Nyahururu, Mwea and any other place within the country where these birds may tend to habit.

What do we do?

our major role is Conservation. We promote the conservation of Grey Crowned Cranes

Activites involved in conservation

Our Major activities involve:
Community Education: for local communities who share the cranes’homes.
Protection: this involves protection of the young ones (colts), eggs and even adults
Identification of nests, eggs and potential homes: we identify potential homes, eggs and nests to initiate care.
