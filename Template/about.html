About Us

Who we are

We are a community-based organisation of volunteers from central Kenya who help in conservation Kenya’s largest population of Grey Crowned Cranes, which are globally endangered birds. This is done by engaging and improving the lives of our local community who share the cranes’ home around Lake Ol’Bolossat, Identification of nests and eggs, protection against illegal pouching among others

What We Do

  • Identify nests, potential habitats, breeding sites and colts
  • Enforce Care and Protection of the birds.
  • Protect the natural habitats of cranes

Activities

  • Keep breeding and population data of the cranes
  • Offer community education to communities around the birds' habitats
  • Campaign against illegal Poaching
